About Us

Connecting Uganda with the best quality secondhand bales from around the globe.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for affordable fashion, we work tirelessly to bring high-quality secondhand clothes from Europe, Asia, and America straight to Uganda. Each bale is carefully selected to ensure our customers get the best variety and value for money.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to fashionable clothes at fair prices. By importing and distributing secondhand bales, we support sustainability, save the environment, and help our community thrive.
